The list of Digital Humanities journals

This list contains the Digital Humanities journals that have been retrieved through automatic extraction and manual curation. The list is available to download on Zenodo.

The metadata are: a numerical identifier, electronic and print ISSN (when it exists), the title, the URL, and a column describing the level of digital humanities publications it stores.
The DH level field may have four values:

  • Exclusively (the journal is completely devoted as publishing Digital Humanities articles)
  • Significantly (a significant set of publications deal with Digital Humanities)
  • Marginally (only a small set of publications are Digital Humanities)
  • Mega-journal
